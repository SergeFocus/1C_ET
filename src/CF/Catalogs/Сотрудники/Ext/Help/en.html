Staff Directory

Card file to the company's employees Payroll.

Position, phone, email, address, passport number, date of birth, INN - Optional details.

Payroll - Table which should be filled with "charges" the employee, specify the period of validity, if not bezsrochnye, size and currency charges, the number of shifts in the calculation of salaries. Rate - 1 shift. The change may be an hour / day / any other period that will umnazhatsya "size" in the calculation payroll.

Types of payroll:

  • Bet
  • For change
  • The percentage of the sum of the implementation (of the enterprise)
  • The percentage of the amount of personal sales (according to the documents Costs of Goods)
  • The percentage of the profits (of the enterprise). It uses a consolidated gross margin
  • The percentage of the cost of work performed (on documents Execution)
  • For a complete set. For his work in the assembly / razukomplektatsii
  • For services rendered during the (number of services)
  • The percentage of the amount of services provided during the
  • Other (for example, compensation for documents or inventory costs during the work)
