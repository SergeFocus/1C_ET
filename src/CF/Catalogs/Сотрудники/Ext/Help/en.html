Payroll - Table, which should be filled with "charges" the employee, specify the period of validity, if they do not bezsrochnye, size and currency charges, the number of shifts in payroll. Rate - 1 shift. Change may be an hour / day / any other period that will umnazhatsya "size" accrual basis of payroll.

Types of payroll:

  • Rate
  • Per shift
  • Percentage of the implementation (of the enterprise)
  • Percentage of total private sales (documents Fuel Products)
  • Percentage of the profits (of the enterprise). Used consolidated gross profit
  • Percentage of the cost of work performed (for Execution of documents)
  • For a complete set. For his work in picking / razukomplektatsii
  • For services rendered during the time (number of services)
  • Percentage of the amount of services rendered during the
  • Other (for example, compensation for documents or for inventory costs for the performance of work)
