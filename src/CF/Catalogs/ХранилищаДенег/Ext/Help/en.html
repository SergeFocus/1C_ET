Directory "STORAGE money"

Designed to account for the money in different stores (offices, bank accounts)

For each repository should specify: Form of payment Currency (If there is an accounting currency) and other details.

Recommended storage in the name of money just to mention currency and form of payment.

Storage recharge automatically debit money from another store in the absence of the required amount in the vault. In this chain of storage replenishment does not work. Storage replenishment can only be with the same currency. Replenishment mechanism works in the spending document Money Flow and disposal of the document receipt of the goods.
