Directory Repositories money

It is intended to account for the money in different stores (cash, current accounts)

For each storage should specify: Form of payment Currency (if you keep records on currencies), and other details.

It is recommended to store the money in the name just to mention currency and form of payment.

Storage recharge automatically write off the money from the other storage in the absence of the required amount in the vault. This chain of warehouse replenishment is not working. Store replenishment can only be with the same currency. Replenishment mechanism operates in spending in the document cash flow and disposal in the document receipt of the goods.
