If the database on the workplace will benefit some employees, it is not superfluous to determine their role. To work with this configuration of users, their passwords and their roles are defined in the mode Configurator 1C:

Must be a user with the role "Full rights"To adjust the program. Install required password for that user. All further basic settings of the program are made on its behalf.

The role of "Realization"It's a role with limited rights only for recording sales.
The role of "Implementation and acceptance of goods, setting prices and rates"Has a more extended rights. Such a user draws the acquisition, regulates prices.
The role of "Treasurer"Operations with money and wages.
The role of "The operator assembly"- For sites on the set (assembly).
The role of "The operator offices"- For the operator of the cash desks.

The role of "Client Company"- For users of the representatives of the actual end-customer business.
The role of "Supplier companies"- For users of the representatives of the suppliers of goods and services for the enterprise.

The role of "System Administrator"- For the system administrator (DBA enterprise to perform routine maintenance on the database.
The role of "Operator function"- For users with a specific function, such as with an interface specified by an additional connection processing.
