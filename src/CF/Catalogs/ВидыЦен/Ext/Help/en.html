To set the prices of goods for independent kinds of prices, you can use Documents Placing Price, Record rates of cards nomenclatureAs well as in Incoming goods documents. Initially, these prices can be set in Registration documents Adjustments and residues.

Dependent prices are registered automatically when you change the prices of goods, on which they depend. Adjustment dependent prices manually is not recommended, since the automatic conversion price "will disappear." If you need pereopreodelit prices and all are dependent on them - use mechanisms Document Set Price.

There are two ways of calculating the dependent price:

  • The percentage of the price (which at 100% - equity prices).
  • The calculation of dependent prices The formula for calculating the price of the base type (if the program enabled subsystem the use of formulas).

Dependent price may also rounded in the calculation, if the flag "price of whole units" or "price tenths". Maybe round Only in a big way.

You can check how sensitive the price will be formed directly on the card type prices. You can register dependence kinds of prices in both directions. Thus, contrary to the price of goods calculated at the price of this type of installation, and vice versa - the prices of this type are calculated when setting the price of the opposite type prices. In this case, the user should ignore the warning about attempts to install the program costs for dependent types of prices. To "set" price sensitive is formed by the sum of the component prices.

Dependency can be determined by sviska sources. In this case, the allocation will be made during the installation of the new price from any of these sources.
