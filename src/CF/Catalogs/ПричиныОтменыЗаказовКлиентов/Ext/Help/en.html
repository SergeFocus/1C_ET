Reasons for the cancellation of customer orders

Directory Reasons for cancellations for the classification of the reasons for the cancellation of the ordered items. The reason for the cancellation may be filled for a particular commodity item. As a reason for the cancellation may be recorded as the cause of the cancellation on the initiative of the customer (purchased from a competitor) and the reasons for which the company was not able to ensure the sale of goods (not available). The reason for the cancellation is filled in the tables of this document
