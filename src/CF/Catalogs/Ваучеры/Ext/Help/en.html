Directory Vouchers

Turns regulated in configuration settings.

Directory allows you to add several types of accounting vouchers:

  • Identifiers counterparties (personal card)
  • Card with a predetermined percentage of customer discounts
  • Card with a predetermined amount of the discount the customer
  • Map storage "points" for client operations implementation
  • Card, redeemable at the amount of sales ("gift") with a specific nominal value

For most organizational operations with vouchers provided special treatment.

The vouchers are recorded as separate items directory.

However, there may be associated with elements nomenclatureWhen it should take into account the actual operation on the acquisition, transfer, extradition and reception vouchers (as a document of strict accountability). For this nomenclature it is desirable to select a folder. To what extent are the vouchers when purchasing goods from emmitenta (manufacturer, supplier cards) or the implementation of the client (such as "Gift Card").

This physical accounting vouchers can be disabled in the settings.

Vouchers can be limited to a period of action, only apply to the selected group or nomenclature nomenclature groupsLimited to warehouses implementation.

Vouchers can be determined by its own bar code.

To study the history of the voucher is a separate report.
