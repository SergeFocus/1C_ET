Directory of contracts with contractors

It is intended to account for settlements with contractors, sales and procurement in the context of individual contracts.

Party to the agreement is Client or Supplier. On the other hand, the contract may be awarded to Organization.

Treaty allow restrictions to issue:

  • in the amount of settlements
  • the number of purchased / sold goods
  • the sum of the emerging debt (on condition that the documents will be registered in chronological order)
  • set strict prices on goods (as set in the register price suppliersWhen used)
  • establish a strict term of payment (the delay)
  • specify% commission
