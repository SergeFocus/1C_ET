Directory "of contracts with contractors"

Designed for accounting with contractors, sales and purchases by individual contracts.

Party to the contract is Client or Supplier. On the other side of the contract can be concluded with Organization.

Treaty allow limitations issue:

  • in the amount of mutual
  • on the amount purchased / sold goods
  • amount arising on debt (assuming that the documents will be recorded in chronological order)
  • set strict Prices for goods
  • establish a strict payment period (delay)
  • set% commission
