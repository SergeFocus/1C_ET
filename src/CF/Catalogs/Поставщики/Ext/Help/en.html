Directory of Suppliers

Designed for filing suppliers. Use if including the integration of suppliers.

Provides predefined Supplier "Private person" for the registration of non-personal data on acquisitions. That is, those for which there is no need to keep track of debt. This provider can be renamed.

Directory hierarchy. That is, suppliers can be grouped.

Requisites:

Type Price (if included posistema Complex mechanism considering the price) determines the price of default for the posting of the goods from the supplier. Price type can be assigned the same for the entire group of suppliers.

Area (if including accounting Regions) determines the location of the supplier. On this basis, you can group Suppliers reports.

Address.Phone.OKPO.Legal status.E-mail, details of cashless payment - Additional information about the suppliers.

Stock provider (When the subsystem), "Accounting for the stock," allows you to specify the default storage for suppliers. Then, when choosing suppliers in the documents, a warehouse will be installed automatically.

Name Print - The text to be displayed in the "Supplier" when printing documents from the program. This text may differ from the usual name of the supplier. For example, the name of the supplier - Sail, Ltd., and to print the name - LLC "Sail", Odessa, Malaya Arnautskaya 9.

Home Currency (if included Accounting for currency) is set for the documents in the selection of the provider automatically sets a particular currency.

Own staff (if included payroll accounting) can act Supplier. Such providers are created automatically when you enter information staff. It is necessary to take into account the settlements with the supplier at payroll employee.

Manufacturer - (if the account is included) A reference to the whole range of manufacturer, purchased from the supplier. The same should be the case when he is the manufacturer Supplier nomenclature. Then, in the statement "Goods. Balances and transactions"It is possible in the version of the report" by producers "to study the movement of his product. Also in Sales Report provided the option "nomenclature sale by producers."

Note that if the program to keep records of suppliers, but not lead account money, In the analysis Report "According to the calculations with suppliers" the picture will look one-sided. As if the goods were bought but not paid for, as there is no possibility to register in the program payment provider with disconnected registered money.

When it is necessary to offset the debt between the supplier and / or Customer Services taking into account the receipt / outflow of money, you should use a special form of adjustments "Offsetting debt counterparties".

Contractors - general name for customers and suppliers.

At the command "Print" you can get a card with the Supplier and its current debt instruments.
