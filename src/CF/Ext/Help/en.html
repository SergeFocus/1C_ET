Configuration for the 1C accounting for 8 outlets for sales of piece goods.

Primary advantage - it's light, with no extra tools and interface elements. Working with her ​​is simple and fast. The operator can prepare quickly.

Configuration is intended for registration of trade accounting for a small, small and very small enterprises (pans, stalls, small shops) or a network of them.

Basic principle in its development was the"elemental"approach to accounting units of the enterprise. These "elements" can be switched on and off depending on the needs of the enterprise or of a given outlet.

Konfiguraitsiya implemented so that no additional configuration is immediately ready for registration of the "primitive" operations on the sale of goods. Next owner can complicate the configuration records in those places where it is needed.

Be sure to be somebody with the role of "full rights" to set up the program. Install the required password for that user. All further adjustments to the program are made ​​by booting on his behalf.

5. Having provided all the necessary settings, work with the program should start by registering indicators and balances. If the company is not "new" - it must be fully described for the program. To do this, a special type of document - "Adjustments and Registration Remnants."
