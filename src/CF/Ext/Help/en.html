The configuration for the 1C accounting for 8 outlets for selling piece goods.
The main advantage of the configuration - it's light, with no extra tools and interface elements. Working with her is simple and fast. Operator can be prepared quickly.

Configuration is intended for registration of accounting in small trade, small and very small enterprises (stalls, stalls, small shops) or a network of them.

The basic principle in its development became "cell"Approach to accounting units of the enterprise. These" elements "can be switched on and off depending on the needs of the enterprise or of the point of sale.
Konfiguraitsiya implemented so that it is out of the box immediately ready for registration of "primitive" operations involving the sale of goods. Then the owner of the configuration can complicate the records in those places where it is needed.

    Functions of "core" documents:

    Consumption of goods and sales of services
    This document is in general to dispose of the goods. It therefore incorporated several types of documents:
    • If you specify a client - a document leave PRODUCTS & SERVICES. If the buyer entered sum payment, it is a document on the full implementation goods and services.
    • If you specify a provider, it disposal goods in connection with the return to the supplier. If entered amount will come from the vendor - a full paper on returning the supplier of the goods and taking money back from the provider.
    • If the counterparty is not specified, it is a document of write-off goods. In this document, the amount of money received, - compensation for cancellation.

    Incoming goods and services
    This document is generally on the receipt, so there are also several types:
    • If you specify a provider - is receiving goods and services from suppliers. If you specify the amount of retirement money - payment to the supplier, this flow of goods. Can be filled with a table of additional associated costs.
    • If you specify a client that is a document on the admission of goods back from the customer. If the specified amount - the return of the buyer and the transfer of the money.
    • If the counterparty is not specified, it is "posting" of goods.

    Monetary movement
    Payment by the buyer to the supplier or payment can be made directly in the document on the implementation and acquisition of goods or services. But often the money is not coming at the same time.
    Document the movement of money, you can register these operations:
    • The arrival of the money from the contractor or other parish money.
    • Flow of money to the counterparty or other cost money.
    • Consumption and the arrival of money in different currencies in connection with one operation. For example, when a customer is calculated in multiple currencies.
    • Currency exchange.
    • Calculation and payment of wages.
