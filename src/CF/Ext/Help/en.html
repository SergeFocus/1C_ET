    Functions of "core" documents:

    Consumption of goods and sales of services
    This document is in general to dispose of the goods.It therefore incorporated several types of documents:
    • If you specify a client - a document leave goods and services to the customer.If you entered the amount of the buyer's payment, it is a document on the full implementation goods and services.
    • If you specify a provider, it retirement goods in connection with the return to the supplier.If entered amount will come from the vendor - is a complete document on returning product supplier and receiving money back from the provider.
    • If the counterparty is not specified, it is a document of write-offIn this document, the amount of money received, - compensation for cancellation.

    Incoming goods and services
    This document is generally on the receipt, so there are also several types:
    • If you specify a provider - is receiving goods and services from suppliers.If you specify the amount of retirement money - payment to the supplier, this flow of goods.It can be filled with a table of additional associated costs.
    • If you specify a client that is a document on the admission of goods back from the customer.If the specified amount - the return of the buyer and the transfer of money to him.
    • If the counterparty is not specified, is "posting" of goods.

    Money Movement
    Payment buyer or payment to the supplier can be made directly in the documents of sale and purchase of goods or services.But often the money comes not at the same time.
    Document the movement of money, you can register these operations:
