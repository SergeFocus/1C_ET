Exchange with sites

Allows you to configure data exchange between the accounting information system and online store that supports protocol between the system "1C: Enterprise" and website.

Key features:

  • Discharge from the program's website catalog of products, including all the properties of the goods, the prices and the remnants of the goods in warehouses, as well as automatic maintenance of the data up to date.
  • Download the program of the order issued in the shop, as well as updating the data on the website of the status of payment and shipment of orders.

It displays a list of configured nodes exchanges with the site

The output data is in the list

Available commands

The output data is in the list
  • Product Name - Hostname
  • date of download - The date of the last download orders from the site
  • Date successful download - The date of the last successful download orders from the site
  • Date of discharge - The date of the final unloading of goods on the site, or update information on orders
  • Date successful landing - The date of the last successful unloading of goods on the site, or update information on orders
  • The exchange of goods - Flag to upload to the site product catalog
  • Exchange orders - Sign download from customer orders

Available commands
  • Run exchange - Starts a session of data exchange with the site
  • Run a full exchange - In this mode the exchange of product catalog data will overwrite the existing directory (if it has already been uploaded to the site)
  • Show registered changes - Opens a form view of goods and orders for which data will be uploaded to the website
