Register "PRICES"

It determines the price at product to type price at currency type price date. Valid until the next price.

Prices are specified manually or via the document "Setting the price".It is recommended to set the prices of nomenclature documents setting prices. But this is not necessary. Just so it's easier to manage. The price can be assigned and entering residues.

Prices Dependent Species Price are calculated automatically.

When you delete a record on the price, remove all corresponding prices dependent species prices.

The price is accounted for in the next second, after that date.
