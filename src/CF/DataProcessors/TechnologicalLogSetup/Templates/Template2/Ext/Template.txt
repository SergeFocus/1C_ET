Name:
Full technological log
Details:
All events with all their properties will be written to the technological log. In this case the technological log might contain huge volume of data, however, it can be useful for analyzing complex abnormal situations. We recommend that you use this template during the test phase and while determining the cause of errors.
Actions:
PathToTL
Template:
<?xml version="1.0"?>
<config xmlns="http://v8.1c.ru/v8/tech-log">
	<log history="168" location="c:\v82\logs">
		<event>
			<ne property="name" value=""/>
		</event>
		<property name="all"/>
	</log>
</config>
