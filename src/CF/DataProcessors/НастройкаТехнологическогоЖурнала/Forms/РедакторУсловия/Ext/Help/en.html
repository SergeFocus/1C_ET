Conditions Editor

This editor includes three related fields:

  • a field in which you select the name of the property that is involved in the condition. It is allowed as an option from the menu, and quick selection by entering a fragment of (in Russian) or identifier (in English) properties. For example, to select properties duration You can enter a fragment durAnd fragment long .
  • selection field conditions.
  • selection field value conditions. In some cases, the selection field value will be a list of choices.

Pressing OK It leads to changes in the conditions of fixing, pressing Cancel - Cancels the changes.
