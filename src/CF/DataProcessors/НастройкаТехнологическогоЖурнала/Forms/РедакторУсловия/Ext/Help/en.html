Conditions Editor

This editor contains three related fields:

  • box in which you select the name of the property that is involved in the condition. Allowed as a choice from the menu, and quick selection by typing a fragment of the representation (in Russian) or identifier (in English) properties. Thus, for the selection of properties duration You can enter a fragment durAnd fragment long .
  • field to select conditions.
  • selection field values ​​of the condition. In some cases the selection field values ​​will be a list of choices.

Pressing OK leads to the fixation conditions change, press Cancellation - Cancels the changes.
