The form of the selection of settings and display objects

The form is designed to modify the selection of settings and display of objects, such as:

  • Displayed information - details columns displayed in the table of found objects.
  • Selection - a collection of objects of selection options.
  • Sort - a sequence of output objects in the table.

To adjust the order of output fields, select the field and added using the corresponding buttons to put them in the appropriate table field.

Setting selection is carried out on the "Selection" from the command bar.
