In this dialog, you can enter a period boundary calculated totals for accumulation and accounting registers.

By default, the following dates are available for setting the boundaries of the calculated results of:

  • registers for storage - the end of the previous month
  • registers for accounting - the end of the month tekueschgo

After the change, the date is automatically converted to the date of the end of the month of the selected date.

If the team set the amount chosen to calculate the results of a single register, it will be able to change only one parameter corresponding to the type of register.
