In this dialog, you can enter a period boundary calculated totals for the accumulation registers or accounting.

By default, the following dates are available for setting the boundaries of the calculated outcomes:

  • for the accumulation registers - the end of the previous month
  • for accounting registers - the end of the month tekueschgo

After the change, the date is automatically converted to the date of the end of the month of the selected date.

If the command setup time calculate the outcome selected for one register, it will be able to change only one parameter, the type of the corresponding register.
