The mechanism of "Register of Sales" for recording transactions for the sale of goods or services to customersone by one in the form of a table in the "non-reclosable box" with major interface elements.


The mechanism can only be of interest in cases where the business process for the retail space looks like a realization of one of the goods or services for a single client.

The configuration provided 5 registration modes sales:
  • Normal (the creation of individual implementation documents)
  • throughinventory
  • Simple sale (Implementation of the registration window is not closed)
  • Register sales (Sale-items to buyers in the form of general table)
  • ARM cashier (in April 2016 is not implemented)
The interface "Sales Register" registration functionality implementation is limited.
Will have to open an ordinary (common) document for the implementation of complex accounting elements.

Thus, for example, by logging mechanism "simple sale"
NOT register:
  • sell more than one product to one client operation
  • take into account the customer contract
  • sell goods and services at a discount
  • to take money from the client when you pay in several different currencies
  • to take money from the client when paying in several forms of payment
  • to take money from the client when paying at the same time in various money vault
  • debit item
  • sell the serial product
  • to sell the goods in additional units
  • return the goods to the supplier
  • register one implementation of the document, but for different customer orders

Work in the "Register of sales" mode is as follows:
Before the user at all times of the table window is open.Where each line - the implementation of a separate document.

The user makes the conditions of implementation - the buyer, a product or service, the payment amount and the program generates a document implementation.

In addition to the form available to the user:
1 - go to the usual list of documents the implementation of key documents and other cash flows.
2 - Choose the period for the table.When the user changes the period - the program reads in the implementation of the operation table.IMPORTANT: The table will only include the implementation of the operation with a single line of the table Products.Other, more complex implementation of the documents can not be read in the table.
3 - Ask the general conditions for the implementation of the document: type of price, storage, private organization, storage of money.
This window is not an independent interface.The user can continue to work with other portions of the database and, if necessary, in the usual implementation of open form.

Form "sales Registry" can be assigned to the user in a form "at the start."

Form "sales Registry" is available for selection as a member of the user's page.
