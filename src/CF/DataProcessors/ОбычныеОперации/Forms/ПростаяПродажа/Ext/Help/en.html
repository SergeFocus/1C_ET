The mechanism of "simple sale" for recording transactions for the sale of goods and services in the "non-reclosable box" with major interface elements.


The configuration provided 5 registration modes sales:
  • Normal (the creation of individual implementation documents)
  • through inventory
  • Simple sale (Implementation of the registration window is not closed)
  • Register of sales (sales in one position for a customer in the form of general table)
  • ARM cashier (in April 2016 is not implemented)
The interface "simple sale" registration functionality implementation is limited.
Will have to open an ordinary (common) document for the implementation of complex accounting elements.

Thus, for example, by logging mechanism "simple sale"
NOT register:
  • sell goods and services at a discount
  • to take money from the client when you pay in several different currencies
  • to take money from the client when paying in several forms of payment
  • to take money from the client when paying at the same time in various money vault
  • debit item
  • sell the serial product
  • to sell the goods in additional units
  • return the goods to the supplier
  • register one implementation of the document, but for different customer orders
