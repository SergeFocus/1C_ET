"The sale of 00016422" Will find the document "sale of goods" with "00016422" No.
"Payment of 120000» Will find the documents "payment" in the amount of 120,000 and 120,000.50
"Ivanov Acacia" This will find all documents drawn Ivanov for the contractor of "Acacia"
"The sale of Acacia" This will find all documents "sale of goods" by the counterparty of "Acacia"
"Acacia 05.07" This will find all documents on the counterpart of "Acacia" of 5 July 2008
"Sneakers" This will find all references to goods such as "sneakers", in order:
• Trainers White (Goods)
• Black Sneakers (Goods)
• The arrival of the goods number 00086822
• Consumption goods number 00016422

Advice:

  • If there was a lot of data, you specify a search query by adding any specification (date, amount, name of the contractor)
  • If there was a little data, simplify the query to exclude from it any word
  • To refine your search using the keywords:
  • Quotes - to search for an exact phrase, such as "a" mini vacuum cleaner ' "
  • OR - Search for either of the two words, such as "acacia or Stroypromvse" - will find all references to the counterparties of OOO "Acacia" and CJSC "Stroypromvse".
  • Brackets - to group expressions, for example, search for "Ivanov (acacia or Stroypromvse)" finds all documents drawn Ivanov Counterparty Ltd. "Acacia" and CJSC "Stroypromvse".
