The configuration for the 1C accounting for 8 outlets for selling piece goods.
The main advantage of the configuration - it's light, with no extra tools and interface elements. Working with her is simple and fast. Operator can be prepared quickly.

Configuration is intended for registration of accounting in small trade, small and very small enterprises (stalls, stalls, small shops) or a network of them.

The basic principle in its development became "cell"Approach to accounting units of the enterprise. These" elements "can be switched on and off depending on the needs of the enterprise or of the point of sale.
Konfiguraitsiya implemented so that it is out of the box immediately ready for registration of "primitive" operations involving the sale of goods. Then the owner of the configuration can complicate the records in those places where it is needed.

Thus, to the primitive trading account, you can add:

  • Record-keeping nomenclature (goods), taking into account
    • Warehouses enterprise. It should, if physically or logically goods companies are in different rooms or the number of the various responsible.
    • Nomenklatura groups. This is an additional information section (group) nomenclature.
    • Manufacturers Nomenclature.
    • Use Barcode - A unique product attributes.
    • Implementation of the "kit" products, which are composed of other products.
    • Maintenance is more preferential price in the sale of an equal or greater number of strips.
  • Complex pricing mechanism. Doing more Species Price set manually or calculated automatically.
    • Mechanisms of formation and accounting of discounts.
    • Record-keeping Gross profits. In that case, if the database recorded the actual price / amount when posting of the sales of goods and an opportunity to examine the volume of gross profit for the enterprise.
  • Accounting for money. In order to accompany the movement of goods information on the accompanying cash flow, as well as to take into account all the other costs and receipt of "Articles".
    • Keeping Monetary accounting. This account allows you to split the money and take account of actual currency, Lead courses Currency exchange views and form of commodity prices.
  • Accounting Customers. With a view of realization becomes personalized. Then you can track individual customers, sales and settlements with them.
  • Accounting Providers. With such a view of the acquisition becomes personalized. Then you can track individual suppliers, the volume of acquisitions and settlements with them.
  • Directory Regions for groups of customers, suppliers or manufacturers.
  • Device Connectivity. For example, to use a barcode scanner. The use of this scanner allows you to quickly generate documents indicate the range in the selection and reporting.
  • Plans sales and the plan-fact analysis.
  • Accounting of salaries Staff. The ability to specify the types of charges and calculate staff salaries for change or for a percentage of the sale or profit. They can take into account the results of the salary InventoriesAs well as the sale of goods to employees, purchase them or settlements with their goods.
  • Additional behavior of the program:
    • Set default values ​​for quick filling elements Card directories and documents.
    • Formula Automotive nomenclature position, forming its own bar code products, text, bar-code (price tag), the calculation of the prices of dependent products.
    • The prohibition or warning in the spending of money is less than zero.
    • The prohibition or warning in spending goods without residue.
    • The prohibition or warning in the expenditure item below the recommended minimum balance indicator.
    • Blocking posted documents, setting the ban on changes in the documents in the past.
    • Check the uniqueness of the names of the Nomenclature, customers and suppliers.
    • The mechanism of label printing products.
  • Subsystem imports and exports data
    • Mechanisms for loading and unloading "caps" and table documents, manuals to exchange information with other databases.
    • Exchange with other instances of the program (upload and download).
  • Logging user actions and retrospectively watch the intensity and efficiency of their work with the program.
