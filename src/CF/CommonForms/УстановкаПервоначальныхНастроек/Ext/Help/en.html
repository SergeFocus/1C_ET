Configuration for the 1C accounting for 8 outlets for sales of piece goods.
The primary advantage - it's light, with no extra tools and interface elements. Work with it quickly and easily. Operator can be prepared quickly.

Configuration is intended for registration of trade on account of the small, small and very small enterprises (trays, stands, small shops) or a network of them.

The basic principle in its development became "elemental"Approach to enterprise accounting units. These" elements "can be switched on and off depending on the needs of the enterprise or of the outlet.
Konfiguraitsiya implemented so that no additional configuration is immediately ready for registration of "primitive" operations involving the sale of goods. Next owner can complicate configuration records in those places where it is needed.

So, to the primitive trading account, you can add:

  • Record-keeping Nomenclature (Goods), taking into account
    • Warehouses enterprise. Necessary if physically or logically Products Company located in different rooms or the number of the various responsible.
    • Nomenclature groups. This additional information section (grouping) nomenclature.
    • Manufacturers nomenclature.
    • Use Barcode - A unique product attribute.
    • Implementation of a "set" items that are composed of other goods.
    • Accompany more preferential price when selling at or above by the number of strips.
  • Complex pricing mechanism. Doing more Kinds of prices installed manually or automatically calculated.
    • Mechanisms of formation and registration discounts.
    • Record-keeping Gross profit. In that case, if the database recorded the actual price / amount when posting of the sale of goods and the opportunity to explore the gross profit of the enterprise.
  • Money account. To accompany the movement of goods accompanying information about cash flow, as well as to take into account all other expenses and receipts for money "Articles. "
    • Conducting Monetary accounting. The inventory is to split the money and are recorded at currencies, Lead courses currency exchange views and form the prices of commodities.
  • Accounting Customers. With such a view of realization becomes personalized. Then you can track individual customers, sales and settlements with them.
  • Accounting Suppliers. With such a view of acquisition becomes personalized. Then you can track individual suppliers, volume acquisition and settlement with them.
  • Directory Regions for groups of customers, suppliers or manufacturers.
  • Connected Equipment. For example, to use a barcode scanner. Using this scanner allows you to quickly generate documents to indicate the range of selections and reports.
  • Sales Plans and plan vs. actual analysis.
  • Accounting salaries employees. Ability to specify the types of charges and calculate staff salaries for change or for a percentage of sales or profits. Their salary can take the results of InventoriesAs well as the sale of goods to employees, purchase them or calculations with them goods.
  • Additional program behavior:
    • Setting default values ​​for quick filling elements card catalogs and documents.
    • Formula for auto-nomenclature, forming their own barcode products, text with barcode (price tag), the calculation of prices of goods dependent.
    • A ban or a warning when spending money is less than zero.
    • A ban or a warning in the spending items without a trace.
    • A ban or a warning in the spending of goods below the recommended minimum balance indicator.
    • Block posted documents, the ban on setting change documents in the past.
    • Check for unique nomenclature, customers and suppliers.
    • Label printing mechanism products.
  • Subsystem imports and exports Data
    • Mechanisms upload and download "caps" and tables documents, handbooks for the exchange of information with other databases.
    • Exchange data with other instances of the program (upload and download).
  • Logging user actions and retrospectively watch the intensity and efficiency of their work with the program.
