Nomenclature selection form with input quantities and prices set at the document.

Depending on where this is caused by the form, you will be prompted Nomenclature, Its quantity and price.

Nomenclature can be specified by Barcode (if used), Article nomenclature (if used), search details and select it from the directory.

By pressing F10 closes the window with the transfer of selected data into the document.

So, for a quick set of goods in the document, you can call this form (F10), scan the barcode, enter the number and return to the document (again F10).

When calling this form of document consumption goodsIf you are using accounting Warehouse Set warehouse and warehouse replenishment, the remainder of the goods shows the sum of residues of these warehouses.
