Document "repricing"

Document is used to register the installation or change in prices for a large list Nomenclature.

Calculation and setting of prices for specific products is convenient to carry a special button "Fill table sets and calculate their prices." In this case, sets the unit price will be calculated from the amounts of all components. In general, the price setting for "set", and the calculation of dependent prices, the program will build on the already formed a set of prices, without having to learn the price of its components.

If you are using a complex accounting prices, the same document set prices for certain types of prices. Just on the "Display information about prices in other forms of prices" can be prepared for the price of one species, based on the value of another form of price.

These "On line" notes, information Polzoatel. Not printed, but are stored in the program.

F10 button available quickly enter dialogue with the amount of product. When using it, just press F10, then enter the scanner or barcode manually (if it is used) Or choose a range, enter the quantity and F10 - complete data entry.

F11 button to enter the barcode manually (if it is used), For example in cases where code scanner is disabled or can not read the code.
