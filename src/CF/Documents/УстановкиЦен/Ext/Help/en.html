The document "SET PRICE"

The document is used to register or change the setting of prices for a large list nomenclature.

The calculation and setting of prices for specific products is convenient to carry a special button "Fill in the table sets and calculate their prices." In this case, the unit price is set will be calculated from the amounts of all components. In general, setting the prices for the "set" and the calculation of dependent prices, the program will build on the already formed a set of prices, without studying the price of its components.

If you use complex accounting prices, the same document set prices for certain type price. Also on the "Display information about prices in other forms of prices" can be prepared for the price of one type, based on the values ​​in another form of prices.

Registered only price change. If the difference in prices - 0, price and dependent on it being recorded.
