The document "Internal Order"

The document is used only when Accounting Enablement Warehouse.

The document is intended to reflect the internal order of the list for the warehouse store on the recipient of the donor.
During the document forms a "requirement."
At the time of receipt of goods to the warehouse of the recipient, the goods order are debited from the "requirements".
If the document is labeled as "made", the product also deducted from the requirement.
Watch the established requirements can be in the report on internal orders.
