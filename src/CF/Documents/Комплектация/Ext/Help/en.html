The document "PACKAGE"

The document is used to register the manufacturing (packaging) or by razukomplektatsiyu specifications products or manually.

On the "Result" - products that complement.

On the "Materials" - written off when complete materials (nomenclature).

On the "works" - works fulfilled staff during the assembly.

On the "Tools" - own facilities companies involved in the set.

On the "Additional expenses" - cash expenses incurred by our company in the process of bundling / razukomplektatsii.

Razukomplektatsiya - the reverse process equipment. In this direction the results are written off, and the materials come back.
