The document "Services OF TIME"

The document is used only whenaccounting Enablement settlements with clients.

The document is intended to reflect the provision of such services to customers who are registered for the time.
The logic of the document is
The operator fixes the start of the provision of services (customer, date, position (number) and (or) means).
Then, at the end of the service is fixed end date and calculated the amount of services rendered, the price is formed.
