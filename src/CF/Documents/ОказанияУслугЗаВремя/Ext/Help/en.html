The document "Services TIME FOR"

The document is used only when Accounting Enablement settlements with clients.

The document is intended to reflect the provision of such services to customers who are registered for the time.
The logic of the document is:
The operator fixes the beginning of the provision of services (customer, date, position (number), and (or) means).
Then, at the end of service fixed end date, and the amount of services rendered, the price is formed.
