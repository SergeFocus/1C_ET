Document "INVENTORY"

  • Perform pre-commissioning and debit products for non-compliance to the actual account.
  • Register the related cash flows or to reflect the difference in wages of employees.
  • Form a linked document sale of goods the inventory results.

    Inventory can not be goods of "sets". Inventory subject to their constituent parts.

    Can not be inventoried items with the tag "without quantifying" and services.

    These "On line" notes, information Polzoatel. Not printed, but are stored in the program.

    Upon reflection of inventory in wages employees primarily considered responsible for the warehouse. If it is not - is distributed among all employees.

    In the configuration settings may be included opportunity to register for the implementation results of the inventory. Such inventory document makes no changes, but is associated with the implementation of the document. For this inventory document should not be strings "posting" of goods (increase in the number), since it is not possible to record a sale.
