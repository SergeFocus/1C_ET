Document "movement of goods"

When using multiple warehouses, Products with a quantitative view of the program should also move from warehouse to warehouse.

Moving with warehouse "goods in transit" is necessary for cases when you need to take into account that is temporarily unavailable due to the lack in such warehouses, from which it can be implemented.

When moving the product type "set" is actually made to move all the elements of its composition. But for a clear account of actual residues still recommended to move each element of the "set" separately. When you move a "set" amount of accounting for its component is automatically calculated for each element. In the table of documents from our ignored.

When using complex accounting mechanisms and price specified in the document type prices, accounting write-off from a warehouse source and warehouse oprimhodovaniya recipient is made by an established price including considering Currency Rates (if applicable accounting currency).
