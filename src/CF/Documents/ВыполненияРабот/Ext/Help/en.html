Document "Works"

The document is used only when Accounting Enablement settlements with clients.

The document is intended to reflect
  • the plan of work (estimate);
  • Customer payment plans;
  • Plan your own related expenses;
  • receipt and delivery of the object of the client;
  • the actual performance of work;
  • actual payment of the Client;
  • the actual costs of its own attendant.
When working on the same subject Client.
First, you can register and collect the data of the work plan, fees, expenses, print estimate.
And then move on to register the actual work, fees, expenses and observe the difference with the planned figures.
In the settings you can use OFF 'because of the scheduling for the Execution of documents.
If you plan vs. actual filling the logic of the document is:
1. identifies the customer, enter the work plan and transferred (sold) the necessary materials (nomenclature), fill out a separate payment plan the client and the associated plan of their own costs in connection with these works.
2. to study the planned result, if it is large enough, - print the estimate to the client.
3. If the client agrees with the estimate, dates and figures are adjusting the plan, and then click "estimate approved."
4. Next, return to this document and notes which were sent on mission work performed upon any date in which the materials were "sold" as a paying customer, any associated costs incurred. You can select multiple rows and "confirm the fact."
5. emerges the result of work (for our company) faktichestky.
