Document of work

The document is used only whenaccounting Enablement settlements with clients.

The document is designed to reflect
  • the plan of work (estimate);
  • Customer payment plans;
  • Plan accompanying their own costs;
  • receipt and delivery of the object of work of the Client;
  • the actual performance of work;
  • actual payment of the Client;
  • actual own associated costs.
